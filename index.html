What is Just-Ed’s Mission?

We strive to support the academic growth of our students, to emphasize the value of experiential learning, and to facilitate students becoming welcomed as members of Canadian society.

Just-Ed is a tutoring service…

Designed to help language learners meet their academic goals by providing guidance, mentorship, and flexible tutoring options to accommodate busy schedules.

How Just-Ed can help you?

We are experienced, qualified, and committed educators that strive to provide subject-specific support to help you improve your study skills, increase your test and assignment scores, and increase your self-confidence.

Benefits of our Services

Subject-Specific Support

University students are challenged by both their busy schedules and the broad requirements of their programs. If you are falling behind on your assignments or are struggling with a particular subject area, we can help! You'll receive individual attention, and we'll adapt to your learning style and pace of learning.

Improved Study Skills

Completing your assigned readings can be challenging in itself, but it's not enough! We'll help you improve your study habits and time management skills so you learn more effectively, in less time. We can also show you how to take better notes and make studying more efficient.

Improved Assignment and Test Scores

We understand that tests, exams, and assignments can cause stress and anxiety that makes it difficult for you to achieve your true potential. We'll work with you to plan ahead, understand key concepts, and pace yourself so you are ready for success. The key is consistency, and we can help!

Increased Self-Confidence

Challenges at school can cause you to feel discouraged, anxious, and frustrated, and can also make it harder to learn new things. We will act as your educational role model and work with you to achieve academic success and improve your self-confidence.
